Located in the heart of Lane Cove on Sydney’s lower north shore, Sloans of Lane Cove is revered best hair salon by the industry and consumers alike with a trove of winning trophies to authenticate their expertise. Led by Owner and Director Scott Sloan, with a personal collection of accolades including Winner of NSW Hairdresser of the Year and 2 x finalist for Australian Hairdresser of the Year, this Sydney salon is a must for those seeking premium hair fashion services.

A Cornerstone of the Community

Celebrating twenty five years in the Lane Cove locality, Sloans of Lane Cove hair salon is a cornerstone of the North Shore community. It is a social salon where local residents have shared special occasions with their trusted local hairdresser for a quarter of a century, and where some of Australia’s top hairdressers have been appointed hair managers to neighbourhood teens who are now top female execs!
